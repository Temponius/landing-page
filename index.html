What Are Pallas Cats?

The Pallas Cat (in reality called the Pallas's Cat or Manul) is a wild cat found around Central Asia, from Iran to Mongolia. They are known for their incredibly dense fur and grumpy facial expression. Pallas Cats live solitary lives in caves, crevices, and burrows. Their diet is mainly Pikas, lagomorphs that look like rodents crossed with rabbits. Pallas Cats are very important for keeping the rodent population in Asia in-check.
